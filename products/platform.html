Capabilities

How It Works

The platform combines AI-driven analysis with structured human oversight to produce thorough, defensible assessment outputs.

Multi-Phase Assessment

Each engagement follows a defined methodology with distinct phases: scoping, automated analysis, finding generation, human review, and deliverable production. No black boxes.

Human Review Gates

AI accelerates analysis but never replaces judgment. Every finding passes through human review gates before reaching the final deliverable. Consultants validate, refine, and contextualize.

GitHub and Jira Integration

Findings flow directly into your issue tracker. Create GitHub issues or Jira tickets from assessment results, with severity labels, epic grouping, and remediation guidance attached.

Framework-Grounded Analysis

Every finding maps to established frameworks: NIST CSF, ISO 27001, MITRE ATT&CK, SOC 2, and OWASP. Auditors and compliance teams get the references they need.

Automated Methodology

Engagement-specific runbooks encode Circle 6 Systems' consulting methodology. The platform ensures consistent, repeatable assessments regardless of which consultant leads.

Structured Deliverables

Assessment outputs follow standardized templates: executive summaries, detailed finding reports, remediation roadmaps, and compliance mapping matrices. Ready for board presentation or audit submission.
