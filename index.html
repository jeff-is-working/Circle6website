Who We Are

Mission-Driven Technology Consulting

Circle 6 Systems is a technology consulting firm built on the principle that the organizations responsible for serving the public deserve the same caliber of technical expertise available to the private sector.

We work alongside state, local, tribal, and territorial government agencies as well as mid-to-large enterprises to solve complex technology challenges. Whether it is hardening an organization against evolving cyber threats, designing systems that scale with growing demands, or integrating disparate platforms into a cohesive operation, we bring the strategic depth and hands-on capability to deliver results.

Our consultants operate as an extension of your team. We invest the time to understand your mission, your constraints, and your operating environment before recommending a path forward. Every engagement is structured to leave your organization stronger and more self-sufficient than when we arrived.

What We Do

Consulting Services

We deliver focused expertise across four core disciplines, each grounded in practical experience with government and enterprise environments.

Cyber Security

Protecting critical infrastructure and sensitive data demands more than compliance checklists. We help organizations build and sustain a security posture that addresses real-world threats while meeting regulatory obligations.

  • Security architecture assessment and design
  • Risk analysis and vulnerability management
  • Incident response planning and readiness
  • Regulatory compliance and audit preparation
  • Security awareness program development
  • Continuity of operations planning

System Design

Sound architecture is the foundation of every reliable system. We work with stakeholders to translate operational requirements into technical designs that are practical to implement, maintain, and scale.

  • Enterprise architecture planning
  • Requirements analysis and documentation
  • Infrastructure and network design
  • Scalability and performance planning
  • Disaster recovery architecture
  • Technology roadmap development

System Integration

Most organizations operate a mix of legacy platforms, cloud services, and specialized applications that were never designed to work together. We bridge those gaps to create unified, interoperable environments.

  • Legacy system modernization strategies
  • Cross-platform data integration
  • API design and middleware implementation
  • Cloud and hybrid environment integration
  • Data migration and transformation
  • Interagency and partner system connectivity

Customized System Development

When off-the-shelf products cannot meet your operational requirements, we design and build purpose-driven solutions tailored to how your organization actually works.

  • Custom application design and development
  • Workflow and process automation
  • Reporting and analytics platforms

  • Ongoing maintenance and support planning

Government organizations and public-serving enterprises face technology challenges that commercial solutions rarely address out of the box. Budget cycles, procurement regulations, compliance mandates, accessibility requirements, and the obligation to serve every constituent create an operating context that demands specialized understanding.

Circle 6 Systems was founded to work within these realities, not around them. We understand how to navigate intergovernmental coordination, multi-stakeholder approval processes, and the documentation requirements that accompany public-sector technology projects.

For enterprise clients, we bring the same structured, methodical approach that government work demands. The result is thorough analysis, defensible recommendations, and implementations that are built to withstand scrutiny and stand the test of operational use.

Our Approach

01
