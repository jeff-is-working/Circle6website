The gap between perceived capability and realized station generates grievance that can be channeled into ideological targets.

Wide Awakes Assessment

The Wide Awakes is a progressive activist network (launched 2020) organized by Amplifier, a Seattle-based nonprofit. The group operates through non-violent art-based activism. Amplifier stated it "had never heard of Cole Allen" and denies any affiliation. Allen's sister described him as "part of The Wide Awakes" but the connection appears to be self-identified affinity rather than organizational membership. This pattern — lone actors attaching themselves to broader movements without coordination — is consistent with decentralized radicalization.

5.2 Ammunition Selection

Allen stated in his manifesto he chose buckshot over slugs specifically to minimize wall penetration. He also expressed hope that security personnel were wearing body armor. This indicates a subject who had rationalized a moral framework around his violence — attempting to limit collateral damage while pursuing primary targets. This is operationally significant: it suggests deliberate research into terminal ballistics and a belief that the attack could be surgically targeted.
